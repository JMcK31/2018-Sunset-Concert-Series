Foxhollow Farm Presents

Sunset Concert Series

2nd Friday of the Month May to September

On the 2nd Friday of each Month form May to September we open our farm to friends and neighbors to enjoy live music, farm fresh food, cold drinks, and good company. The Sunset Concert Series is family friendly and fun for all ages. All shows are rain or shine. Guests bring blankets, chairs, even pets on leash. Tickets include admission and parking. Savory and sweet treats are available for purchase from a variety of local food vendors including: Farm to Fork Catering, Wiltshire Pantry, Duck Duck Beet, LIFEbar, Gelato Gilberto, Steel City Pops and more. Craft Beer from Milewide, craft cocktails, wine and non-alcoholic beverages are available at the Sunset Concert Series bars. For more information visit our About page.

Don't forget to bring home the beef. Pick up Foxhollow Farm's 100% Grassfed Beef at the Beef Booth and learn about how you can have Foxhollow's 100% Grassfed Beef delivered "From our Farm to your Door," with Foxhollow Delivery.
